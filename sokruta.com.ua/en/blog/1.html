Survhi Jalan studio about the tastes of modern design

28.08.2014    

Fulfilling orders like conveyor is not our style of work. On the contrary, we try to find an individual approach to every project and embody both our experience and some new information. In order to keep up with the last tendencies, our company members regularly attend professional events and follow the news.

Also we are engaged in various public activities. The studio participates in national and international contests and takes prizes.



Tweet

We were also involved in ComfortHouse exhibition, which took part from 20th to 23rd of September. Within the framework of the exhibition, architectural and construction forum «Home-2012» - from the project to realization – took place. The contest «Design taste», which was held among the best designers in the country, became the most significant event on the forum. During the contest, 20 famous Ukrainian designers presented their projects on decorating living space of the house territory, and the best one was chosen from them. Judges noted contestants’ creative approach to the tasks and high quality of the works done, despite limited timing of the contest.

Survhi Jalan has played a very important role in the contest’s organization by creating a stylistic conception for decorating the contest and becoming a conceptual redactor on placing the contestants’ installations. Reaction of the contest’s guests gives grounds to believe that the event was held on the high level and the work of our designer was fulfilled in the best traditions of our studio: stylishly, qualitatively and considering modern technologies.

Why we participate in the events

 

To keep up with the events, you need to become their part. This is the principle we are ruled by when choosing the events to participate and to visit. Because the variety of solutions we can offer to our clients directly depends on the width of our outlook.

 

Different forums, seminars, courses and breefings are the best possibility to communicate with the collegues, gain new knowledges, professional advices and gain new idea for developing professional skills.

Design is dinamic. That is why it is necessary to follow its tendencies every day. Getting fresh information from reliable sources is a great option, but personal involvment into the designer events is the best teacher. We should also note, that our studio participates in such events not only as a contest, but also as an organizer.

top

Thanks to personal experience and understanding of modern requirements to the interior design, we can offer our clients smart solutions on decorating their apartments and houses. Modern technologies let the customer see the finished project in the real size and from various angles, which gives the opportunity of estimating the quality of our work in the most accurate way.

Survhi Jalan studio is professionally engaged in interior design considering both aesthetic and practical features of the solution. Our projects are not only a beautiful form, but also a content, which is carefully thought out. Order the interior design project from us and make sure, that the main priority in our work is the client.

The competition attracted 20 designers who provide the best design solutions of living space adjacent territories.
